Department of Energy Redesign

Project Duration

May - July 2022

Project Overview

The goal of this project is to redesing The United States Department of Energy (DOE) website and provide users with a better UI/UX experience. To accomplish this goal, myself and two more designers will work together to conduct interviews and research in order to find out what are the main points of the website. Based on this research, we will create a website prototype that offers a solution to the current website flow.
